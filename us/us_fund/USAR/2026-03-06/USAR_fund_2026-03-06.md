## Chapter 2: Company Overview & Business Model

### 2.1 Company Profile

USA Rare Earth, Inc. was founded in 2019 and is based in Stillwater, Oklahoma. The company engages in mining, processing, and supplying rare earths and other critical minerals within the United States. 

**Employees**: ~30

### 2.2 Business Model Evolution

Currently an exploration and development stage company, USA Rare Earth is aiming to transition into a vertically integrated domestic supplier of critical minerals. Its flagship asset is the Round Top Mountain project located near Sierra Blanca, Texas. The company explores for neodymium, dysprosium, terbium, gallium, beryllium, lithium, and other vital minerals. Its long-term model involves moving up the value chain from raw extraction to separated oxides and ultimately to rare earth magnet manufacturing, filling a massive gap in the US domestic supply chain.

### 2.3 Core Competitive Advantages

1. **Strategic Resource Base**: The Round Top deposit is unique in its high percentage of heavy rare earth elements (HREEs), which are more scarce and critical for high-performance magnets than light rare earths.
2. **Geopolitical Alignment**: Perfectly positioned to benefit from US government initiatives (e.g., Inflation Reduction Act, DoD grants) aimed at securing domestic critical mineral supply chains away from foreign reliance.
3. **Lithium Co-Product**: The presence of lithium in its deposits provides an additional, highly sought-after revenue stream tied to the EV battery market.
