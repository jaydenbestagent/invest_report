## Chapter 2: Company Overview & Business Model

### 2.1 Company Profile

USA Rare Earth, Inc. was founded in 2019 and listed on NASDAQ. The company engages in mining, processing, and supplying rare earths and other critical minerals within the United States. 

**Employees**: ~30

### 2.2 Business Model Evolution

The company operates primarily as an exploration and development stage mining entity. Its flagship asset is its interest in Round Top Mountain near Sierra Blanca, Texas. The company aims to construct a fully integrated domestic rare earth supply chain, from raw material extraction to advanced processing (e.g., neodymium, dysprosium, terbium, gallium, beryllium, lithium).

### 2.3 Core Competitive Advantages

1. **Domestic Sourcing (Geopolitical Moat)**: Offers a secure, U.S.-based alternative for critical minerals essential to EVs, defense, and green energy, avoiding international trade bottlenecks.
2. **Heavy Rare Earth Concentration**: The Round Top deposit contains significant concentrations of heavy rare earth elements (HREEs) and lithium, distinguishing it from conventional light rare earth mines.
3. **Strategic Government Alignment**: Highly positioned to receive grants, favorable financing, or collaborations through U.S. strategic mineral initiatives.
