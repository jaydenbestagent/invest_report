## Chapter 2: Company Overview & Business Model

### 2.1 Company Profile

Centrus Energy Corp. (formerly USEC Inc.) supplies nuclear fuel components and services for the nuclear power industry. It is the only U.S.-owned enrichment supplier capable of meeting both commercial and national security requirements.

**Employees**: ~467

### 2.2 Business Model Evolution

Centrus is transitioning from a fuel middleman/broker into a full-scale primary domestic enricher. While its legacy business involved selling Separative Work Units (SWU) sourced from international partners (like Russia's TENEX), its future lies in the American Centrifuge technology and HALEU production for advanced reactors.

### 2.3 Core Competitive Advantages

1. **U.S. National Security Importance**: Sole domestic source for enrichment services required for tritium production and other defense needs.
2. **First-Mover Advantage in HALEU**: Only U.S. company currently producing HALEU under DOE contract.
3. **Established Customer Base**: Long-term backlog with major domestic and international utilities extending through 2040.
