## Chapter 2: Company Overview & Business Model

### 2.1 Company Profile

Energy Fuels Inc. (founded in 1987) engages in the exploration, recovery, recycling, and sale of uranium, vanadium, rare earth elements (REEs), and heavy mineral sands. It operates primarily in the United States and is headquartered in Lakewood, Colorado.

**Employees**: ~1,069

### 2.2 Business Model Evolution

Historically a pure-play uranium miner, UUUU has rapidly evolved its business model to become a critical minerals hub. By leveraging its unique White Mesa Mill in Utah (the only fully licensed and operating conventional uranium mill in the US), it is processing monazite sands to produce REE carbonates, positioning itself upstream in the EV supply chain alongside its traditional nuclear fuel business.

### 2.3 Core Competitive Advantages

1. **The White Mesa Mill**: A unique, fully licensed asset with replacement value measured in billions and a timeline of over a decade if started from scratch today.
2. **Diversified Critical Minerals Strategy**: Multi-commodity exposure (Uranium, Vanadium, REEs) buffers single-commodity price shocks.
3. **Geopolitical Tailwind**: Aligned perfectly with US government initiatives to secure domestic nuclear fuel and rare earth supply chains away from Russian/Chinese dominance.
