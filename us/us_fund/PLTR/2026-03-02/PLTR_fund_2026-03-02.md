## Chapter 2: Company Overview & Business Model

### 2.1 Company Profile

Palantir Technologies Inc. was founded in 2003 and listed on the NYSE in September 2020 via direct listing. Headquartered in Denver, Colorado, the company was co-founded by Peter Thiel, Alex Karp (current CEO), and others with initial focus on counterterrorism and intelligence applications for government agencies.

**Employees**: ~4,429 (as of December 2024)

**Market Position**: Leading provider of enterprise data integration and AI platforms for both government and commercial clients

### 2.2 Business Model Evolution

Palantir has undergone a strategic transformation from a government-focused defense contractor to a diversified enterprise software platform company:

**Phase 1 (2003-2015)**: Government Intelligence Focus
- Developed Palantir Gotham for counterterrorism and intelligence operations
- Established deep relationships with US and allied defense/intelligence agencies
- Built proprietary ontology for complex data integration

**Phase 2 (2016-2022)**: Commercial Expansion
- Launched Palantir Foundry for commercial enterprises
- Expanded into healthcare, finance, manufacturing, and other sectors
- Developed AI/ML capabilities integrated across platforms

**Phase 3 (2023-Present)**: AI-First Platform Company
- Launched AIP (Artificial Intelligence Platform) in April 2023
- Integrated LLMs into enterprise workflows
- Shifted go-to-market to focus on transformative outcomes vs. incremental use cases
- Achieved GAAP profitability and positive free cash flow

### 2.3 Core Competitive Advantages

1. **Ontology-Based Architecture**: Decades-long investment in data ontology enables seamless LLM integration and enterprise-wide AI deployment—creating significant barriers to entry

2. **Government-Cleared Infrastructure**: Security clearances and established trust relationships with defense/intelligence agencies create moats that commercial competitors cannot easily replicate

3. **Network Effects**: As customers expand usage across their organizations, platform value increases through shared ontologies and cross-functional data integration

4. **Boots-on-Ground Deployment Model**: Embedded forward-deployed engineers ensure customer success and drive expansion revenue through demonstrated ROI

5. **AI Execution Capability**: While competitors focus on AI model development, Palantir excels at operationalizing AI into measurable business outcomes—addressing the "last mile" problem in enterprise AI
